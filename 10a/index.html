Ideas to test for each friction area

Getting acquainted with the team

  • Assign each new hire an onboarding buddy: this will be the go-to person for any major questions
  • Book 1 to 3 "coffee" video chats a day for first week. This will help introduce everyone on the team and give chance for candid 1:1
  • Virtual team "hangout" and icebreaker

Resources, guides, processes

  • Create a centralized repository of onboarding documents including team processes and cadences
  • Create detailed, step-by-step guides for getting setup
  • Create a tight-scope "Starter" project that is executable in 1-2 weeks and that is helpful in learning the design system and product surfaces
